What we do

One shop, the whole process.

From a single custom part to full production runs, our certified team handles fabrication, welding, cutting and processing under one roof.

Custom Fabrication

Handrails, stairs, catwalks, mezzanines, gates and more. ADA-compliant rails and OSHA-approved stairs, with full detailing services.

Welding

Our certified team performs the highest quality work, including portable and mobile welding, heavy equipment repair, aluminum and stainless.

Cutting

Superior cut quality on fully automated and manual saws, plus plasma and oxy-fuel cutting, increasing yield and minimizing drop.

Metal Processing

An assortment of saws and machinery for your processing needs, from single jobs to production quantities on programmable bandsaws.

Metal Sales

Ornamental metal components in a variety of shapes, sizes and materials, for DIY projects and full-service clients alike.

Custom Manufacturing

Drilling, punching, tapping, production welding, weld positioning and sheet-metal bending, plus one-off custom builds.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

What kind of work do you take on?+
Custom fabrication, welding, cutting and metal processing, from a single part to full production runs. Handrails, stairs, catwalks, mezzanines, gates and more, in steel, aluminum and stainless.
Are your welders certified?+
Yes. Our team includes AWS D1.1 certified welders, certified in a variety of materials and processes, and we build to ADA and OSHA standards.
Do you offer mobile welding?+
We do. Our certified welders can perform work at your site, including heavy equipment repairs, or in our shop, whichever suits the job.
Can you handle large projects?+
Absolutely. We are set up for projects of any scale, from concept through completion, with the machinery and certified team to back it up.
