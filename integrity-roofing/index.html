Good to know

Roofing questions, answered.

+Do you really inspect roofs for free?
Yes. We offer a free, no-obligation roof inspection. Our team gives your roof a methodical, top-to-bottom look and an honest read on its condition, whether you end up needing work or not.
+Can you help with my insurance claim?
Absolutely. We started in 2017 specifically to help homeowners through the insurance claim process after storm damage. We document the damage thoroughly and guide you through each step.
+Do you offer financing?
We do. Financing is available so a new roof doesn't have to wait on your budget. We'll walk you through options that keep your home protected now with a plan that fits.
+Do you handle both homes and businesses?
Yes. Integrity Roofing works on both residential and commercial roofs, from single-family homes to commercial buildings across the region.
+What areas do you serve?
We're based in Hickory, NC and serve the Piedmont, Western North Carolina, the Catawba Valley, and the greater Charlotte region. If you're nearby, give us a call.
