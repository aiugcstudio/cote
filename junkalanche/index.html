Questions, answered.

What kind of junk do you take?

Just about anything non-hazardous. Furniture, appliances, mattresses, household clutter, yard and construction debris, and full clean outs. If you are not sure about an item, give us a call and we will let you know.

How much does junk removal cost?

Pricing is fair and based on how much we haul and the type of job. We give you an upfront quote before we start, so there are no surprises. Call or text for a free estimate.

Do I need to move everything outside first?

No. Our trained crew does all the heavy lifting, wherever the items are, from the basement to the attic to the back yard. You point, we carry it out.

How big a job can you handle?

Any size. From a single item pick up to large commercial and construction cleanouts, we complete projects of every scale across the region.

Which areas do you serve?

We are based in Waterford and serve Oakland, Genesee, and Livingston Counties throughout Southeast Michigan.
