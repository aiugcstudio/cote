Good to know

Questions, answered.

What kind of properties do you work on?

We are a full-service exterior contractor for residential, multi-family, and commercial properties. Whether it is a single home, an apartment building, or a commercial site, we can handle the exterior work.

What exterior services do you offer?

Roofing, siding, exterior painting, exterior repairs, window and door installation, decks and patios, coatings and sealings, and fencing. We also offer consulting packages for larger projects.

What is a consulting package?

It is for homeowners and property managers planning a larger exterior project. Depending on what you need, it can include a roof inspection, a siding estimate, or full exterior planning so you know the scope before work begins.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Estimates are free, and we typically respond within one business day. Call, text, or send us a message and we will get back to you to set up a time.

Which areas do you serve?

We are based in Salem and serve Keizer, Albany, West Linn, Lake Oswego, Wilsonville, and the surrounding Willamette Valley.
