Good to know

Answers before we get started.

Do you really offer a free inspection?

Yes. We start every relationship with a free, no-obligation inspection. We identify what you are dealing with, explain your options honestly, and only recommend what you actually need.

What pests do you handle?

General household pests, mosquitoes, termites, cockroaches, ants, fleas and mites, plus spiders, wasps, rodents, and more. If something is bugging you, we can take a look.

How often should I have my home treated?

For most homes, a regular, proactive plan keeps pests out year round. During your inspection we will recommend a schedule that fits your home, your concerns, and the season.

Are your treatments safe for my family and pets?

Keeping your family safe is the whole point. We apply treatments carefully and thoughtfully, and we are always happy to walk you through what we use and how to prepare.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Gainesville and serve homes and businesses throughout North Georgia. Not sure if you are in our area? Give us a call and we will let you know.
