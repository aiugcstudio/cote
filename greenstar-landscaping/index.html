Good to know.

Answers to the questions we hear most.

When can my children and pets go on the lawn?+

With granular fertilizer, you can go on the lawn right after the application. With liquid weed control, let the material dry on the grass first. Drying time varies with the weather, but is roughly four hours.

When should I water my lawn?+

Water in the early morning for about 15 minutes, three days a week, depending on the weather. After a new lawn application, wait 24 hours before watering. Avoid watering at night, as it can increase disease activity in the lawn.

What happens if it rains on my scheduled date?+

Rain happens every year. When it does, we simply push the schedule forward to catch up. If your day is Wednesday and Tuesday gets rained out, we come Thursday, then resume the normal schedule the following week.

Do you guarantee your work?+

If something isn't right, talk to the crew on site or reach out to Jason within the first five days of completion. Because our vendors don't warranty plant material to us, we can't guarantee plants once they're installed, but we stand behind our workmanship.

Do you offer snow removal?+

Snow removal is offered to a limited client base who have held full-service landscape services with GreenStar for two years.
