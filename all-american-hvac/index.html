Services

HVAC, refrigeration, and plumbing, done right.

All American HVAC & Plumbing Services repairs, installs, replaces, and maintains residential and commercial air conditioning and heating systems in Rainbow City, Gadsden, and Southside, AL.

Air Conditioning

As a local residential and commercial AC repair service, we get more calls for air conditioning problems than any other service. We're the experts when it comes to evaporators, coils, air handlers, chillers, and AC compressors, on call 24 hours a day to find the cause and fix it properly.

Heating

When temperatures drop, you count on your heating system to kick in. Boilers too. We'll come out any time of day or night, troubleshoot the problem, and get your furnace back up and warming your home in no time. If it's beyond repair, we can install a brand new one.

Commercial Refrigeration

Grocery stores, restaurants, hotels, and convenience stores depend on refrigeration that works at all times. We repair, replace, and maintain refrigerated cases, ice machines, ice cream machines, walk-in freezers, reach-in coolers, and display cases.

Plumbing

Our professional plumbers specialize in repairs, replacement, installation, and maintenance. We're the 24/7 emergency plumbers in Rainbow City: drain cleaning, pipe leak repair, water and sewer line repair and replacement, and water heater service. BBB A+ Rated.
