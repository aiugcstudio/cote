Know your rights

You choose the shop that repairs your car.

Some insurers may tell you that you have to use a "preferred shop." That is not true, and it is against the law. The choice of who repairs your vehicle is always yours.

Your legal right

You have the right to choose your own repair shop, no matter what an adjuster suggests.

No "preferred shop" rule

An insurer cannot force you to use a specific shop. That claim is untrue and unlawful.

Yes. We accept all insurance carriers and handle the complete claim for you, with inspections completed right here at the shop so the process stays simple.

Do I have to use the shop my insurer recommends? +

No. You have the right to choose your own repair shop. Some insurers may say you must use a "preferred shop," but that is untrue and against the law. The choice is always yours.

Are estimates really free? +

Always. Estimates are free, and every completed repair also includes a complimentary detailing of your vehicle.

What kind of repairs can you handle? +

Collision and cosmetic repair, unibody and frame straightening, specialized MIG welding, and carbon fiber, aluminum, and steel structural repairs, plus sales and installation of aftermarket parts and accessories.

Will my car look the way it did before? +

That is the goal on every job. Our certified technicians and factory-grade equipment are used to return your vehicle to pre-accident condition or better.
