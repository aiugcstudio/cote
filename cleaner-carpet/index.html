What areas do you serve?+
We serve Siskiyou County across Northern California, including Yreka, Montague, Gazelle, Lake Shastina, Weed, Mount Shasta, McCloud, Dunsmuir, Scott Valley, Seiad Valley, Happy Camp and Klamath River. If you are nearby, just ask.
What can you clean?+
Carpet for homes and businesses, carpet repairs and stretching, upholstery such as sofas, chairs and pillows, and tile and grout on floors, countertops, walls and showers.
Are your cleaners safe for pets and kids?+
Yes. Our upholstery and carpet cleaning uses products that are safe for both people and pets, so the whole family can enjoy the room right after we finish.

Absolutely. We repair pet tears, burn marks and split seams, and we re-stretch carpet that has developed wrinkles or waves so it lies flat again.
