What we do

One team for the whole project.

A full spectrum of services, from expert demolition and eco-friendly salvage to comprehensive construction. Whatever the project needs, from the ground up, we handle it.

Demolition

Eco Shield Texas provides full-structure demolition and disposal services for residential and commercial properties. From small sheds to large commercial buildings, our team ensures safe and efficient demolition, adhering to the highest standards of safety and precision.

Selective Demolition

Our selective demolition services are ideal for remodeling and remediation projects. Whether it's interior or exterior work, Eco Shield Texas offers meticulous and controlled demolition, preserving the integrity of surrounding structures while meeting your specific project needs.

Construction

At Eco Shield Texas, we handle all aspects of general construction, from initial planning to final execution. Our experienced professionals are committed to delivering top-quality results, ensuring your construction project is completed on time and within budget.

Site Prep & Foundations

Clear and prepare your site for success with expert grading, excavation, and groundwork. Strong foundations are essential, and our team provides reliable foundation work for any structure.

Extensions & Remodeling

Expand or revamp your space with our skilled remodeling team, plus strong, precise framing, durable roofing for new builds and remodels, sturdy patio covers, and interior and exterior painting.

Scrap & Salvage

Eco-friendly scrap removal and salvage to minimize waste, recycling unwanted material responsibly at every stage of the project.
