How it works

A simple process, a spotless finish.

Every job follows the same careful steps, so you get thorough, consistent results whether it is one room or an entire building.

Step 01

Inspect

We walk the space, note problem areas, traffic lanes, and stains, and set the right approach.

Step 02

Pre-treat

Spots, high-traffic paths, and set-in soil are pre-treated so they release cleanly.

Step 03

Deep clean

Hot-water extraction flushes out embedded dirt, allergens, and odors down to the base.

Step 04

Dry & protect

We groom the fibres, speed drying, and can add protection to keep it fresher for longer.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

What areas do you serve?
We are based in Indianapolis and clean for homes and businesses across the city and surrounding communities, including Carmel, Fishers, Greenwood, and more. If you are nearby, just ask.
Do you handle both homes and businesses?
Yes. We have served both residential and commercial customers since 1999, from single rooms and sofas to offices, storefronts, and recurring commercial floor care.
What can you clean besides carpet?
Along with carpet, we clean upholstery, tile and grout, and air ducts, plus area rugs, pet stains and odors, stain protection, and commercial floor care.
How long does carpet take to dry?
Drying time varies with the fabric, humidity, and airflow, but most carpet is ready to walk on the same day. We groom the fibres and help speed things along before we leave.
Can you help with pet stains and odors?
Absolutely. Pet accidents are one of the most common things we treat. We target both the stain and the odor so your space feels genuinely fresh again.
