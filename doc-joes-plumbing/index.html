Half a century of trust

A household name in the Inland Empire since 1975.

Doc Joe's was established in May of 1975 by Joe Widmer, who quickly became known as The Sewer Doctor. That original cartoon doctor with his bag and plunger still rides on our trucks and uniforms today.

After the Harbin family grew Doc Joe's into a respected residential and commercial name, current owner Sean Berg was honored to become the new Sewer Doctor. He has carried the legacy forward with cutting-edge technology and a highly trained team, while keeping the same promise: exemplary workmanship and the highest standard of customer service.

What we do

From a slow drain to a whole repipe.

Fifty years of residential and commercial plumbing, with the equipment, manpower and expertise to get your problem resolved quickly.

Sewer & Drain Cleaning

24 hour drain cleaning for kitchen, bath, floor and main sewer lines, clearing everything from grease to tree roots.

Water Heaters

Install and repair of traditional and tankless heaters from AO Smith, Rheem, Rinnai, Noritz and more, with long warranties.

Hydrojetting

High-powered water jetting that washes away years of grease, sludge and debris, and cuts through the toughest roots.

Trenchless Sewer Relining

A less invasive way to repair sewer lines from the inside, with a service life of 50 years and financing available.

Leak Detection & Repipe

Electronic leak location, water-damage response, and full copper or PEX repipes to protect and add value to your home.

Water Treatment

Whole-house filtration, softeners and anti-scale systems for cleaner, better water throughout your home or business.
