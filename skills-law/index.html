What is Skills.law?

SKILLS.law offers virtual and in-person educational programming aimed at equipping our community with the knowledge, mindset, skills, and tools needed to thrive in AI, Data, KM, innovation, legal operations, and transformation roles across the legal space.

Who should join?

SKILLS is geared towards those who hold roles in AI, Data, KM, innovation, legal operations, and transformation across law firms, legal departments and LegalOps.

Why attend?

SKILLS events provide a peer-to-peer experience for attendees, combining engaging educational content with interactive discussions, aimed at building valuable connections between industry leaders and peers in law firms, legal departments, and LegalTech.

SKILLS events are free to attend for law firms and legal departments.
