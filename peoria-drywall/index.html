Good to know

Questions, answered.

What areas do you serve?+
We serve Peoria and communities across Central Illinois, including East Peoria, Bartonville, Bloomington, Canton, Dunlap, Pekin, Metamora, Galesburg, Eureka and more. If you are nearby, just ask.
Do you do both drywall and painting?+
Yes. We handle drywall repair, hanging, finishing and installation, along with interior and exterior painting, plaster, skim coating and wallpaper removal, so one team can take a room from bare to finished.
What makes your epoxy floors different?+
Every metallic epoxy floor is poured and marbled by hand, so no two are the same. They are seamless, easy to clean and tough enough for garages, basements and commercial spaces.
Do you take on small jobs?+
Absolutely. No job is too small, from a single patched ceiling to a touch-up before you list your home. We show up and get it done.
Can you handle water or fire damage?+
Yes. We repair drywall and ceilings after water and fire damage, and finish the work so it matches the rest of the room.
