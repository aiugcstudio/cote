What we arrange

Lending built around collateral and cash flow.

We specialize in creative solutions for our clients. We are innovative and quick to assess any type of financing that has collateral and cash flow. We welcome inquiries if your acquisition, repositioning, bridge, or refinancing needs are $25K to $1B.

Fix & Flip Loans

Direct correspondent lender for small-balance SFR ($50K to $2MM) and Multifamily ($50K to $5MM) fix-and-flip loans, 100% LTC and 100% renovation costs.

Bridge & Rental Loans

Bridge loans, rental loans, and rental portfolio loans structured to your acquisition, repositioning, or refinancing needs.

Commercial Loans

Ground-up construction, SBA, USDA, Agency, Conventional, CMBS, bridge, and term commercial loans.

Working Capital & ABL

Working capital, asset-based lending, factoring of accounts receivable, and equipment and inventory loans.
