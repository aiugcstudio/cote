Incorporating Solar

Turn your roof into long-term savings.

A commercial roof is a large, flat, sun-facing asset. KENTEX incorporates solar directly into the roofing scope, so the panels and the roof are planned together instead of fighting each other later.

The result is a roof that keeps water out and cuts your energy bill down, engineered as a single system from the deck up.

Good to know

Commercial roofing questions, answered.

Do you only work on commercial roofs?+
Yes. KENTEX Roofing Systems is focused on commercial roofing for property managers and building owners in the Austin area, from single-tenant buildings to multi-tenant properties.
Can you add solar to our existing roof?+
We incorporate solar as part of the roofing scope so the panels and the roof are planned together. That protects the roof, simplifies the install, and sets your building up for long-term energy savings.
What kind of metal roofs do you install?+
We install standing seam metal roofs in both architectural and structural profiles, with 1", 1.5", and 2" seam heights, specified to fit your building and its span.
Do you offer maintenance plans?+
Yes. Our preventative maintenance plans put your roof on a scheduled inspection cycle, and our annual roof reports document condition and priorities so you can plan and budget ahead of a problem.
How do you handle quotes?+
We start by getting to know you and your building and assessing the roof properly, then give you an honest and fair quote. You will always know what is happening and why, from first look to final walkthrough.
