What we do

Everything your chimney needs, under one roof.

From an annual sweep to a full rebuild, we keep your fireplace safe, clean and running the way it should, all backed by CSIA certification.

Sweeping & Inspection

Annual maintenance that removes hazardous creosote and soot and catches any problem early, so your fireplace stays safe and efficient.

Chimney & Masonry Repair

From cracked mortar and crowns to full structural rebuilds, we restore the integrity of your chimney and protect your home from leaks and deterioration.

Sales & Installation

Upgrade your home with top-quality fireplaces, stoves and inserts from our selection, paired with expert installation.

Relining & Waterproofing

New liners, crowns, caps and waterproofing that seal your chimney against water damage and keep it drawing safely for years.

Dryer Vent Cleaning

Tuckpointing & Rebuilds

Firebox repairs, tuckpointing and complete chimney rebuilds, done clean, quick and efficient with a lasting result.

Our story

From K-9 handler to chimney expert.

After six years as a full-time officer and deputy, four of them as a K-9 handler, Logan Roddy wanted a career that gave him more time with his wife Teresa and their five children.

He and Teresa saw a need in Northwest Arkansas for a chimney company built around a genuinely high level of customer service. In July 2021 they started Reliance Chimney, and Logan earned his CSIA certification to back it up.

That same discipline runs through every job today: show up on time, do what you say, and leave the home cleaner than you found it.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

How often should I have my chimney swept?+
For most homes that burn regularly, once a year. An annual sweep and inspection removes hazardous creosote buildup and catches small issues before they become expensive or dangerous.
Are you certified?+
Yes. Reliance Chimney is a CSIA Certified Chimney Sweep through the Chimney Safety Institute of America, and we carry an A+ rating with the BBB.
Do you repair masonry and rebuild chimneys?+
We do. From cracked mortar, crowns and caps to tuckpointing, firebox repair and full rebuilds, we restore the structure and seal it against water damage.
Do you install fireplaces and stoves?+
Yes. We sell and install quality fireplaces, stoves and inserts, and we clean dryer vents too.
