Good to know

Questions, answered.

What services do you offer?+

Anything related to the plumbing, heating, or cooling systems in your home, plus ventilation, water treatment, water filtration, well systems, and full bathroom and kitchen remodeling. If it involves water or comfort in your home, we can help.

What areas do you serve?+

We're based in Portsmouth, NH and serve Seacoast New Hampshire and Southern Maine, including Dover, Durham, Exeter, Hampton, Rochester, Rye, Stratham, Kittery, Eliot, Berwick, and the surrounding towns.

Are you licensed and insured?+

Yes. Pettigrew Plumbing & HVAC is a family owned business that has been fully licensed and insured and serving the Seacoast since 2001.

We do. Because we keep every type of plumber on staff, we handle everything from a single repair to new construction and complete bathroom or kitchen remodels, all with the same standard of workmanship.
