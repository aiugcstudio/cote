Services

Care built around your brain.

An evidence-based and integrative approach to neurological testing and recovery, tailored to how your brain actually works.

Functional Neurology

Functional neurology is an evidence-based and integrative approach to neurological testing that examines balance, neural timing, brain imaging, and oculomotor function to assess brain, vestibular, and balance issues. It identifies disruptions that MRIs can miss.

Low-Level Laser Therapy

Treat anxiety, fatigue, chronic pain, hair loss, acne, and more with low-level laser therapy. Clinical studies have demonstrated that LLLT can reduce pain intensity, inflammation, and promote tissue healing, enabling patients to recover faster and often with a reduced need for medication.

Autoimmune Disorders & VOMS Testing

Integrative support for autoimmune disorders alongside VOMS testing, part of a whole-brain assessment of vestibular, ocular, and balance function to uncover the root of the problem.
