FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Get in touch
You already built my site? How?

We pull from what's publicly out there: your Google reviews, your listed services, photos of your work. Then we design and write a full site around it. If we sent you a link, that's what's behind it. Anything wrong or missing gets fixed before launch.

What does $297 a month actually cover?

The site itself, hosting, security, your domain connection, and unlimited edits handled for you. One flat rate. Add-ons like the AI chat agent or SEO are separate and entirely optional.

Do I own the website?

You own your domain, your content, and your leads, always. The design and system are ours while you subscribe, which is how we keep it at $297 instead of a $5,000 build fee. Cancel and we hand over your content cleanly.

What if I already have a website?

Then you'll see yours next to ours and decide in about 10 seconds. If your current site is winning, keep it, genuinely. Most were built years ago, and it shows.

Is there a contract or minimum term?

No. Month to month, cancel whenever. We'd rather earn the next month than lock you into it.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Service businesses of every kind: trades, clinics, salons, studios, firms. If customers search for you before they call you, this is built for you.
