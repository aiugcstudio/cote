Who we are

Real strength training, the useless crap left out.

SNF Fitness is a Personal Training & Strength Studio, not a gym. Clients from an array of backgrounds and ability levels meet with us in an appointment-based manner to focus on the important parts of a proper strength training program and nutrition protocol.

Our fully open floor plan combines strength, suspension, functional, core-specific, and traditional gear, in a space designed to make everyone comfortable, confident, and able to maximize their time with us.

Train with us

Four ways to work with a trainer.

Every option includes a custom program, guided out-of-studio work, full nutritional guidance, and unlimited phone, email, and text support through the week.

01

1-on-1 personal training

The most traditional form, one client and one trainer. A custom program built around your gym days, both in studio and on your own, with our full nutritional program included.

02

Semi-private training

Everything that comes with a standard package, with multiple clients sharing one trainer's time and knowledge, each running their own program. Not a group class.

Around 30% off 1-on-1 rates
03

Couples & buddy training

Train alongside a partner or close friend with one trainer and save. Same style of program as a 1-on-1 setting, designed to accommodate two people working toward shared goals.

04

Private group training

Built for teams, friends, co-workers, or family, from 4 to 8 people who share a goal. Same backend guidance and nutrition, at a fraction of the cost. Directed by a trainer, never a bootcamp.
