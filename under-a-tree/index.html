Leadership

Meet Amy McDonald.

Principal & CEO of Under a Tree, Amy leads the firm's work in business definition, feasibility, and concept and design development, with a focus on regenerative approaches to land stewardship, community integration, and built environments.

Her philosophy centers on designing wellness businesses that honor the land, celebrate local culture, and create lasting benefits for the communities they inhabit. She works across Europe, Asia, South America, Mexico, and Canada with owners, family offices, and developers on next-generation wellness destinations and regenerative hospitality communities.

Amy is a member of the Global Wellness Institute's Wellness Real Estate Initiative and received the Leading Woman in Wellness award from the Global Wellness Summit in 2024.
