Our craftsmanship

The name is a promise.

A Straight Up Fence Co was built on doing things right. Straight posts, level rails, square corners, and materials chosen to stand up to the coast. No shortcuts, no crooked lines, no callbacks.

Brian and the crew treat every fence like it borders their own yard. We measure carefully, set posts solid, and leave the site clean, so the fence looks as good in ten years as it does the day it goes up.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

+Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. We come out to your property, walk the line, talk through your options, and give a clear, honest quote at no cost.
+What types of fences do you build?
Wood and redwood, vinyl, chain link, ornamental iron and aluminum, ranch and horse fencing, privacy fences, and gates including automatic gates. We also handle repairs and post replacement.
+Do you work with both homes and businesses?
We do. A Straight Up Fence Co handles residential and commercial fencing across San Luis Obispo and the Central Coast.
+What areas do you serve?
We are based in San Luis Obispo and serve the surrounding Central Coast, including Los Osos, Morro Bay, Pismo Beach, Arroyo Grande, Atascadero, Paso Robles, and nearby communities.
+Can you repair an existing fence?
Yes. Whether it is a few leaning posts, a damaged section, or a gate that no longer swings true, we can repair it or replace what needs replacing.
