Meet the Managing Member

Denzel R.B. Cadet, Esq.

Founder & Managing Member, Cadet Legal

Denzel brings nearly a decade of experience representing private companies in complex commercial transactions across a range of industries. Before launching Cadet Legal, he developed his skills at Wall Street law firms in New York and served as a corporate associate in the Washington D.C. office of a California-based global law firm.

His work spans forming startups and managing financings through to billion-dollar M&A deals and private equity transactions, advising asset managers, fund sponsors, boards of directors, and portfolio companies with personalized guidance at every stage.

The son of a Haitian immigrant father and a mother from West Philadelphia, Denzel is highly proficient in Spanish, mentors aspiring professionals, and appears regularly on industry panels discussing entrepreneurship, venture capital, innovation, and intellectual property strategy.
