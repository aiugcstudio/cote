Meet the Managing Member

Denzel Cadet, Esq.

Founder & Managing Member, Cadet Legal

Before launching Cadet Legal, Denzel was a corporate associate in the Washington D.C. office of a California-based global law firm. He previously worked as an associate and law clerk at a New York-based international firm handling finance and corporate matters.

His experience spans forming startups and managing financings through to billion-dollar private equity transactions, providing personalized guidance through finance and corporate matters, and a commitment to helping clients achieve their goals.
