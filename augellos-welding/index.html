What we do

Pontoon work done the right way.

Bob and his crew have repaired hundreds of pontoons at the Lake. No other shop delivers the same quality, value and professionalism.

Pontoon Inspection

We pull the side skirts and underskin, then pressure test the entire pontoon and inspect it visually to find every crack, hole and weak point. Flat rate of $260.

Repair & Reinforcement

Leaks, dents and design flaws welded and repaired to the highest quality, with added reinforcements the factory never fitted so the problem stays fixed.

Dock Pickup & Delivery

We collect your boat right from your dock and return it there once the repair is done, or meet you at a designated lake access ramp.

Docks & Trailers

Aluminum and steel welding for docks, boat trailers, skegs and lower units, built and repaired to hold up on the water.

Biminis & Fabrication

Bimini frames and custom aluminum, stainless and steel fabrication. If you can think it, we can build it.

Backed 5 Years

Every repair is guaranteed for five years. If a repair leaks or breaks, we fix it free of charge. Estimates are always free.

Our story

The expert marinas turn to.

In 2011, Bob Augello moved to the Lake of the Ozarks in Sunrise Beach and opened Augello's Welding and Fabrication, right off Highway 5 on Lake Road 5-35.

With more than 21 years of welding experience, Bob is the welder that marinas turn to as the expert in his field. He and his experienced staff are dedicated to their craft, dedicated to their service, and dedicated to the satisfaction of the customers they serve.

Hundreds upon hundreds of pontoons have been repaired here, and there is no other shop at the Lake that delivers the same quality, value and professionalism.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

What does a pontoon inspection involve?+
We remove the side skirts and underskin to expose the top of the pontoon, then pressure test the entire pontoon along with a full visual inspection to locate cracks, holes and design flaws. The inspection is a flat rate of $260.
Do you pick up my boat?+
Yes. We can pick your boat up right from your dock and return it there once the repair is complete, or meet you at a designated lake access ramp.
Are your repairs guaranteed?+
They are. We guarantee our repairs for five years. If a repair ever leaks or breaks, we will repair it free of charge.
What else do you weld?+
Beyond pontoons we work on docks, trailers, skegs, lower units and biminis, and we handle custom aluminum, stainless and steel fabrication.
How do I get an estimate?+
Call or text us at (573) 207-0285, or email augelloswelding@gmail.com. Estimates are always free.
