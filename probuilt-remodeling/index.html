Good to know

Questions we hear a lot.

What areas do you serve?
We are based in New Britain and work throughout the Greater Hartford area and nearby Connecticut towns. Not sure if you are in range? Give us a call and we will let you know.
Do you handle the whole project or just part of it?
As a general contractor, we can manage your entire remodel and coordinate related work so it all comes together. Bathrooms, decks, porches, framing, windows, roofing, siding, and sunrooms are all in-house.
Are estimates free?
Yes. We come out, look at the space, talk through what you want, and give you an honest, detailed estimate at no cost.

Open communication is one of our core values. We give regular status updates so you always know where your project stands and what is happening next.
