Good to know

Questions, answered.

What roofing materials do you install?+
We install shingle, slate, tile, metal and EPDM roofing, along with siding. Because we work across all five systems, we can recommend the material that genuinely suits your building rather than defaulting to one.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve Northeast Ohio, from Ashtabula and Medina Counties to Cleveland, Canton, Akron and the surrounding communities, based out of Twinsburg.
Do you handle large commercial projects?+
Yes. We have installed and restored roofs on landmark buildings across the region, including work at the Cleveland and Akron Zoos, and we offer full project management for complex builds.
Can you take on steep or difficult roofs?+
Absolutely. Steep and challenging roofs are a specialty of ours, and it is part of why we have earned a reputation for the toughest installs in Northeast Ohio.
How do I get an estimate?+
Call us at 330.425.2478 or email info@usaroofinginc.com and we will arrange an inspection and give you a clear, honest estimate.
