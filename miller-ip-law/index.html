Will I actually work with an attorney?

Yes. When you schedule a consultation you chat directly with Devin Miller, an experienced attorney, not an assistant or a law student.

What does the flat fee cover?

Everything for the service you choose is bundled in: attorney time, draftsman and illustration fees, government filing fees, and paralegal work. You approve a quote before anything starts, so there are no hidden costs.

Most questions get a reply within 30 minutes, 90% of the time, and always within 24 hours.

Trademarks typically file in about 1.5 to 2 weeks and patents in about 3 to 4 weeks. Government review timelines run separately after filing.

No. The firm was built for startups and small businesses, the clients other firms skip over. We serve all 50 states and world-wide.
