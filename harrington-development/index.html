Common questions

Good to know.

A few of the questions we hear most from builders, developers, and property owners across north Georgia.

What does a site contractor actually do?

We handle the groundwork that comes before anything gets built: clearing the land, grading it to plan, digging and moving earth, and preparing pads, drives, and drainage so the site is ready for construction.

Do you work with builders and developers?

Yes. A lot of our work is for builders and developers who need a reliable crew to clear and grade lots and move a project forward. We also work directly with property owners on their own land.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Canton, Georgia and work on site projects across north Georgia. If you are not sure whether your project is in range, reach out and we will let you know.

Can you handle clearing and grading for a new build?

Yes. Clearing raw land, rough and finish grading, and shaping building pads is core to what we do. We can take a lot from wooded and untouched to graded and ready.

Do you do erosion control and drainage?

Yes. Erosion control, silt fencing, and drainage are part of doing site work properly, keeping water and soil managed so the site holds up over time.

How do I get a quote?

Send us a few details about your project through the form below. We will follow up, and when it makes sense we will come walk the site so the quote is based on real ground, not a guess.
