About Invision

Built on transparency and execution.

A boutique development model, redefined for South Florida

Founded on the principles of transparency and excellence, Invision has redefined the boutique development model in South Florida.

Our mission is to develop high-end residential real estate through disciplined underwriting, conservative capital structures, and institutional execution, delivering transparent, asset-backed investment opportunities aligned with investor objectives.

Invision was founded by Renato Lerner, a civil engineer with over 30 years of experience leading and expanding construction and development companies across three countries.

  • Invision's philosophy is built on one principle: capital stewardship
  • Conservative underwriting, controlled execution, and transparent reporting
  • We invest alongside our partners, ensuring incentives remain aligned throughout the lifecycle of each development
  • Transparent, asset-backed investment opportunities aligned with investor objectives
