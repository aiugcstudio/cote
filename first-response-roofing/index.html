Good to know

Commercial roofing questions.

Do you only work on commercial buildings?+
Yes. Commercial roofing is our focus, from office and retail to warehouse and industrial property. It is a different job from residential, and it is the work we are built for.
Can a coating really save my roof from replacement?+
Often, yes. If the roof still has good structure and is not too far gone, a restoration coating can seal leaks and add years of life at a fraction of a full replacement. We assess honestly and only recommend it when it makes sense.
How fast can you respond to a leak or storm damage?+
Rapid first response is the whole idea. Reach out and we will get on the roof quickly to limit the damage and lay out a plan, including temporary protection when it is needed.
What roof systems do you work with?+
Flat and low-slope commercial systems including TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, built-up, and metal, plus coating and restoration systems. We match the system to your building and how it is used.
What areas do you serve?+
We are based in Indianapolis and serve Indianapolis, Fishers, and Bloomington, along with the surrounding Central Indiana communities.
