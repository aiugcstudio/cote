About Bill Lambert

Nearly 50 years, spent where it holds or fails.

Bill Lambert brings nearly 50 years of experience in the architectural building products industry. He has collaborated with manufacturers, architects, and developers across the U.S. and Europe, identifying the structural weaknesses that quietly affect organizational performance.
