Our point of view

Organizations behave like buildings.

  • A weak foundation eventually shows
  • Poor structure leads to execution failure
  • Lack of reinforcement causes collapse under stress
  • Blocked openings choke communication and flow
  • Sloppy finishes damage reputation and retention
