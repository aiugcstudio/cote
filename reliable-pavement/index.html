What we do

One firm managing your pavement project end to end.

Most property owners go straight to a contractor and hope for the best. RPS is the step before that: an independent consulting and project management firm working exclusively on the owner's side.

Unbiased Consulting

Your independent consulting and project management partner, working only for the property owner.

Scope of Work Development

Independent site assessments and detailed, specification-driven scopes so every bid is measured against the same standard.

Competitive Bidding

At least four qualified bids from our pre-vetted subcontractors, giving owners real choice and a fair investment.

On-Site Oversight

Daily supervision from pre-construction through completion, so the work matches the specification you paid for.

Reserve Fund Planning

Helping HOAs plan pavement work around budget cycles so major repairs never come as a surprise.

Documentation & Reporting

Daily progress reports, photos, inspection updates, and permanent completion records for every project.

The RPS standard

Built to fill a void in the industry.

RPS started in 2012 to fill the void of accountability, competency, and integrity in the pavement industry. Founder Rick Scheibley brings over 32 years of hands-on experience in grading, paving, pavement maintenance, and seal coat work, and served as Director of Paving for more than 18 years before building RPS to work only for the owner.

  • At least four qualified, pre-vetted bids on every project
