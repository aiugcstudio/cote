Who we are

Two decades of quality flooring, built on care.

R & J Flooring LLC is owned by Roberto Hernandez and based in New Haven, Connecticut. Since 2004 we have handled flooring projects across the state, from a single room to a full home or commercial space.

We take care of everything, from the pre-installation inspection right through to the finished floor. That meticulous, start-to-finish approach is why homeowners and businesses across Connecticut keep calling us back.

What we install

Every surface for every room.

Whatever look you are after, we help you choose the right material and install it to last. Here are the floors we work with most.

Hardwood

Solid hardwood with timeless character, installed cleanly and built to be refinished for decades.

Luxury vinyl plank

Durable, water-resistant LVP that mimics real wood, ideal for busy homes and high-traffic spaces.

Laminate

Affordable, hard-wearing laminate in a wide range of styles that stands up to everyday life.

Tile & stone

Precise tile and stone work for kitchens, baths, and entryways, set true and grouted to last.

Carpet

Soft, comfortable carpet for bedrooms and living areas, stretched tight and finished neatly.

Engineered wood

Real wood veneer over a stable core, a smart choice for basements and changing conditions.

How we work

From the first inspection to the final board.

We handle every stage ourselves, so nothing gets rushed and nothing gets missed. Here is how a project comes together.

01

Inspection

We inspect the space and subfloor first, checking for moisture, level, and anything that needs attention.

02

Measure & quote

We measure carefully and walk you through options and a clear, honest quote before any work begins.

03

Prep

We prepare and level the subfloor properly, because a lasting floor starts with what sits beneath it.

04

Installation

Our crew installs your flooring cleanly and precisely, then leaves the space tidy and ready to enjoy.
