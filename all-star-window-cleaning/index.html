Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do you clean both inside and outside windows?

Yes. We clean the interior and exterior of your glass in the same visit, including doors and sidelights, so every pane is clear from both sides.

How do I get an estimate?

Just call or text us at 480-688-7177. Estimates are free, and we will give you a clear, honest price for the work with no obligation.

Do you work with businesses as well as homes?

We do. Alongside residential window cleaning, we handle storefronts, offices, and commercial buildings across the East Valley on a schedule that suits you.

Can you remove hard water stains?

In most cases, yes. Hard water spotting and mineral buildup are common in Arizona, and we use the right approach to lift them and restore the clarity of your glass.

Which areas do you serve?

We are based in Gilbert and serve Phoenix's East Valley, including Ahwatukee, Apache Junction, Cave Creek, Chandler, Gold Canyon, Fountain Hills, Mesa, Paradise Valley, Queen Creek, Scottsdale, Sun Lakes, and Tempe.
